## The Walk East

> Three days through the wetter parts of the swamp. **Húxiān** kept the pace and the conversation, and **Leaf** kept the direction. The closer they got, the quieter the swamp became.

>> 1. By the third day, the wildlife was gone. No bird-calls. No insects. No frogs at dusk. Even the air felt held.

## The Ruins

> The Twilight Ruins were a small temple complex that had sunk by half into the wetland, walls choked with dead vegetation and a single weathered statue still standing at the centre.

>> 1. **Heron** lit a torch out of habit and the flame guttered, blue at the edges, before steadying.
>> 2. There was no flower. There was a body.

## Atrix

> A young woman in ceremonial robes lay on a slab of mossy stone before the statue. Her eyes were open. Her chest did not rise.

>> 1. **Heron** crossed himself in the manner of his temple and **Bereket** stepped forward to confirm what they all already knew. The body was **Atrix's**. The girl who had whispered Leaf into being.
>> 2. **Húxiān** went pale. *"That is not... she should not be..."*
>> 3. The body had no wound. It had been emptied. Whatever made Atrix Atrix had been pulled out of it cleanly.

## Nith Returns

> The torch went out. The air at the centre of the ruins folded inward, then unfolded, and a woman the party had last seen on the Isle of Nith was standing among them.

>> 1. **Nith M'aera** did not announce herself. She looked at Atrix's body the way a cat looks at a mouse it has already eaten, and then at the party.
>> 2. *"Messengers. Still in one piece. Almost a shame."*
>> 3. **Bereket** put an arrow into the space where she was standing and the arrow passed through and out the other side without effect. **Heron** raised his shield and his voice and called on Dyrena.
>> 4. The goddess did not bother to fight them. She walked through the line, brushed past **Leaf**, and slid a hand into Leaf's chest as if into water. The hand came back with a small ember between two fingers.
>> 5. Leaf folded down at the knees and was, suddenly, just leaves.
>> 6. *"A loan from a sister. Returned."* Nith stepped sideways and the ruins kept her place for a moment, then forgot her.

## Aftermath

> The party stood in a circle of dead vegetation around two bodies that had been alive five minutes earlier in two different senses.

>> 1. **Húxiān** gathered Leaf's larger pieces into the cloak Atrix had given them. **Heron** wrapped Atrix's body in his own travelling cloak and could not look at her face for long.
>> 2. The clay jar Inda had given Heron was still empty.
>> 3. They turned west and walked, with what they carried, in silence.
