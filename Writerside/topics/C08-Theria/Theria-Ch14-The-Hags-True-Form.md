## Back to the Cabin

> The cabin had not moved, but the lights inside it burned brighter than the party remembered. **Lady Inda** opened the door before **Húxiān** had whistled.

>> 1. *"You are alive. All of you. I am surprised. Come in."*
>> 2. **Heron** carried Atrix's body across the threshold. Inda's face did not move when she saw it. She gestured to a long table she had cleared in the centre of the room and motioned for him to lay the body there.

## The Blossom Handed Over

> Heron unsealed the clay jar. The Atrix Blossom was still alive, still warm.

>> 1. Inda lifted it out with two fingers, set it in a different vessel, and looked at it for a long moment.
>> 2. *"This will do. You did well."* The compliment had the texture of an apple given to a horse.

## The Dragon

> Húxiān asked her mother, in Common so the party could follow, whether the green dragon that had stopped them on the way home was anything she should know about.

>> 1. Inda's eyebrow climbed. *"A young one bothered you? They are usually more careful than that. I will have words."*
>> 2. *"My mother is a hag,"* the dragon had said. The party put their two pieces together and waited.

## The Reveal

> Inda saw the moment land and did not bother to hide from it.

>> 1. *"Yes. Did you think a small woman in a swamp cabin healed you with herbs?"* She took a single step back from the table and the air around her stopped pretending. She was taller. Her skin went blue, then bluer. Her eyes grew bright and yellow and her voice carried weight that the cabin did not quite contain. *"I am a hag. I have a coven. I have been here a long while. I have helped you because I chose to, and I will continue to choose to, as long as our interests stay aligned."*
>> 2. **Heron** kept his shield arm where it was. **Bereket** did not draw. **Quack** drank a steady-mind tonic without ceremony.
>> 3. Húxiān stepped between her mother and the party, not blocking, mediating. *"You can sit down, all of you. She is dramatic. She is not about to eat anyone."*
>> 4. Inda's form softened back to the grandmother shape after a moment, but her eyes stayed brighter.

## Breakfast Refused

> Inda offered breakfast. The party declined, politely, in unison.

>> 1. *"Wise,"* she said, without offence. *"Húxiān, feed them from your own stores. We have business to discuss."*
>> 2. She lifted Atrix's body in arms that had no business being able to and walked it out the back door of the cabin, toward the trees and a structure the party could not see from where they stood.
