## The Tunnel

> The rough tunnel stretched ahead, flickering with a distant light. Brother Elias raised his shield and led the party deeper into the cave.

>> 1. The flicker at the far end suggested a chamber, lit. The party advanced quietly under Elias's drifting orb of light.
>> 2. Roughly forty feet from the entry shaft, the floor gave way beneath Elias's foot.

## The Pit Trap

> Elias fell forward into the opening. His arm reached for the far side but caught only enough leverage to twist him in the drop. A brief screech of metal on metal, then a wet sound that wouldn't easily leave anyone who heard it.

>> 1. Twenty-five feet below, **Brother Elias was dead**. His body pinned by jagged spears jutting from the bottom of the pit. His eyes stared open at the ceiling. His glowing orb reflected in the small pool of blood forming beneath him.
>> 2. The pit spanned about seven feet across, with narrow ledges either side.
>> 3. No one upstream came to investigate the noise. **Vuulla**, it seemed, had stayed on the party's side.

## What Could Be Saved

> The party debated leaving the body to the spikes, then decided to recover what they could from Elias's effects before pressing on.

>> 1. **Bereket** set an anchor with his rope and climbed down to the lieutenant's satchel.
>> 2. Recovered: a small **owl figurine** (the messenger bird token), a vial of **antivenom** labeled as such, a drawn portrait of a woman wrapped in leather, a **+1 longspear**, and a **steel shield**.
>> 3. Lost to the fall: a broken vial of healing, a soaked and shredded scroll, and a note rendered unreadable by blood.
>> 4. Elias's armor was too damaged to salvage. The body would have stayed on the spikes regardless: too risky to move quietly, and the spears might have been tipped.

## Crossing

> A seven-foot gap, narrow ledges either side, and a pit of spears beneath. Nobody wanted a second body in there.

>> 1. **Heron** jumped the gap first, carrying a length of rope across to anchor for the others.
>> 2. **Ali** shimmied along one of the narrow ledges. **Waldemar** crossed with steady footing. **Bereket** crossed last and recovered the rope and grapple for the road ahead.
>> 3. The tunnel continued from the far side, the lieutenant's drifting orb still hovering over his body below.
