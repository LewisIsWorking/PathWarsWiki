## Atrix, Body and Soul

> Inda returned an hour later with empty arms and an expression that was, for her, almost soft.

>> 1. *"Atrix's body is with my sisters. The Blossom is the seed she left behind. Her soul, Nith does not have. We will work to bring her home."*
>> 2. The party did not press her on the *we* or the *home.* Inda made a tea no one wanted to drink and sat with them at the table.

## The Three Threads

> She had work for them. Three threads to pull on, while her coven prepared the rest.

>> 1. **An island in the Shivering Strait.** The resting place of a long-dead God. Something there was needed for the rite. She would not name the God. She would name the island only to Heron, in private, when the time came.
>> 2. **The Dragon's Maw, in Noveroke.** A temple over a waterfall, with a jewelled stone door. Behind the door, an arrangement of items the coven needed. Lethal to lone visitors.
>> 3. **Hwa-Lun.** The kitsune empire of Húxiān's people. The Emperor sat on an ancient power source recovered from the Frozen Wastes. The coven wanted a piece of that power, and Húxiān was the only person in the room who could approach the court without being skinned.

## Húxiān's Face

> Húxiān went quiet at the third thread.

>> 1. *"Mother. Hwa-Lun has not been kind to our family. I have not been home in years for good reasons."*
>> 2. *"I know,"* Inda said, and did not soften. *"You will not be alone."*

## A Pause Before the Walking

> Inda gave the party two weeks. The swamp was patient and the cabin had room. **Quack** rebuilt his kit. **Bereket** worked on the bow he had been carrying since the arena. **Heron** prayed and slept and prayed again. **Húxiān** taught the others a few words of Hwa-Lun Common.

>> 1. Inda took Heron aside twice in those two weeks, and would not say what she had said to him after.

## The Pull

> On the morning of the thirteenth day, Heron felt something behind the sternum. A tug. South-southwest, persistent, the kind of feeling that did not let go when he tried to set it aside.

>> 1. He told Bereket first. Then Húxiān, who recognised the shape of a god's hand on him.
>> 2. Inda confirmed it without surprise. *"Atrix watches from where she can. She would not pull a faithful man toward something she did not want him to find. Follow it."*
>> 3. *"South is on the way to one of your threads anyway,"* she added. *"Go and answer what is calling. Come back to me when you are done, and we will choose the next thread together."*
