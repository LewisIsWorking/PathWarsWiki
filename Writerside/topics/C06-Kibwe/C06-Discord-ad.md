## Kibwe — Pathfinder 2e Play-by-Post 

> The Story. 
>> 1. The jungle city of Kibwe is rotting from the inside. 
>> 2. A slime curse seeps through its streets, its shrines, and its people. 
>> 3. You came to stop it. Whether the city survives is up to you.

**The basics**
- System: Pathfinder 2e. PF2e and SF2e content such as classes and ancestries are welcome.
- Level: you start at 7 and the campaign wraps at level 8.
- Setting: Golarion, with homebrew changes
- Format: play-by-post, constant but slow
- Open slots: 6
- Cost: free. I pay for Foundry, players pay nothing.

**Where we play**
- Narrative play-by-post lives in our Telegram group.
- Dice, maps and tokens run in Foundry. I will get you set up, no purchase needed.

**Building your character**
- Play almost anything. Any ancestry, class or background is on the table.
- Dual class is allowed but never required.
- PathBuilder is the easy way to put a sheet together.

**Good to know**
- This is one ongoing, connected story with a persistent group, not a West March.
- Tone is adult and high fantasy: dark, magical, and not shy about mature content.
- Vetting happens on the Path Wars Discord, not in my DMs.

New players are welcome to DM me to join and I will happily walk you through everything.
You can also reach out to our campaign helpers @mrnegetz and @nemesiux.
