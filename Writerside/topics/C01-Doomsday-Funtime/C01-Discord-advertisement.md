# C1-Doomsday-Funtime-discord-ad.

## Title: Doomsday Funtime (live voice game).
> This can be a lot of information, but you do not need to read all or any of it. Feel free to DM me, and I will walk you through everything.
>> 1. I like putting everything down in writing so new players have it all to refer back to.
>> 2. System: PF2e.
>> 3. Open Slots: 6.
>> 4. Time & Frequency: Fridays (main session, live on the voice channel), sometimes Sundays (roleplay & shopping session, completely optional).
>> 5. Time zone: UK. Friday time = 20:00.
>> 6. Campaign Description: Stop the world from ending. The party travel the world to discover what will cause the end of times, while discovering more about themselves.
>> 7. THIS IS NOT A WEST MARCH. THIS IS NOT A PLAY BY POST, it is played live on the voice channel. THIS IS NOT A PAID GAME.
>> 8. Age: 18 or older.

> Where does the group play?
>> 1. The Platform the group plays on is Foundry, you do not need to buy anything; I will help you get set up, and it is totally free for players.
>> 2. The group uses Discord for voice/video calls, and Telegram for texting and out-of-session play-by-posts.

> Info.
>> 1. Setting: Golarion.
>> 2. Level: 11. The campaign will go to level 20.
>> 3. I recommend using Path Builder to build your character.
>> 4. What can I play regarding race, class, background, etc? Well, you can play anything.
>> 5. Current party composition: Champion/Oracle, Barbarian/Witch, Magus/Witch and Exemplar/Gunslinger.
>> 6. You do not need to make your character a dual class, but you can if you want to.
>> 7. House rules: we run Ancestral Paragon (a free bonus ancestry feat). I'll help you switch it on in PathBuilder.
>> 8. The vetting process is on our Discord server, **it is NOT in my DMs with you**.
>> 9. New players are welcome to DM me to join. :slight_smile:
>> 10. @PF2e LFG

> You can also reach out to our campaign helpers:
>> 1. @mrnegetz
>> 2. @nemesiux
