# Ophelius-Berrus

> Overview
>> - Campaign: C07-Hopeful-end-times.
>> - The masculine form of Ophelia, from Greek "ōphéleia" meaning "help/benefit").
>> - A dwarf surrounded by the tick-tick-tick of hundreds of chronometers.
>> - Analogue clockwork pieces with spinning gears alongside sleek digital displays.
>> - The timepieces cycle through galactic standard time, Akitonian sols, and stranger measurements. 
>> - He sells timepieces and weapons?
>> - Wears a monocle over one eye, and a mechanical arm ending in a multi-tool.
>> - Wears a long coat with many pockets, each containing various tools and gadgets.
>> - Has a workshop filled with clocks, gears, and strange devices.
>> - Known for his punctuality and precision.
>> - Speaks in a measured, deliberate manner, often using time-related metaphors.
>> - Has a reputation for being able to fix anything, no matter how complex.
>> - Has a mysterious past, with rumours of being involved in time-related experiments.
>> - The shop is called "Castle-Counting".
>> - Castle Counting is a reference to Castle Korvosa.
>> - Ophelius is the father of Gervald Berrus & Damrock Berrus.
>> - Ophelius Berrus will be a shopkeeper throughout Campaign 07.

> History.
>> - The DF party freed Ophelius in the Gauntlight.
