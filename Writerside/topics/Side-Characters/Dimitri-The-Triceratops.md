# Dimitri the Triceratops

**Bonded to:** Kieran
**Campaign:** #6 Kibwe

---

## Origin

Dimitri was a beast of burden belonging to a human merchant on one of Kibwe's wide streets.
The merchant painted garish crimson and gold stripes along Dimitri's frill in the style of Kibwe's merchant houses.
The merchant beat Dimitri regularly with a weighted crop.

When the merchant transformed during the slime curse outbreak, the triceratops panicked.
Dimitri snapped his harness and trampled the remains of his former master.

## The Bond

During the White Flame Slime encounter, Kieran stood between Dimitri and danger.
This was the only kindness the triceratops had ever known.

After the battle, Kieran attempted to shoo Dimitri away.
The triceratops refused to leave.
It pressed its frill gently against Kieran's chest and fell into step behind him.

Kieran named him Dimitri.
He wiped the garish paint from the triceratops's frill.

Kieran placed some of his slime on Dimitri and asked Eda to keep an eye on the dinosaur.
The slime sank into the triceratops and glowed green momentarily.

## Current Status

Dimitri follows Kieran everywhere.
When Kieran speeds up, Dimitri speeds up.
When Kieran slows, Dimitri slows.

Dimitri cannot fit inside buildings.
He waits outside.
