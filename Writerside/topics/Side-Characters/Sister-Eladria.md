# Sister Eladria

## Overview

> Sister Eladria is a young acolyte at the Temple of Pharasma in Raven Grow.
>> - Cleric of the Lady of the Graves.
>> - Record keeper of the temple.
>> - She has taken her first spiral vow but has not yet received the Grave Blessing.

## Appearance

> Early twenties. Grey acolyte robes with a hood, usually pushed back.
>> - Dark hair pinned neatly. Ink stains on her fingers more often than not.
>> - Wears a small silver spiral pendant, simpler than Father Vauran's heavy chain.
>> - Carries books and papers almost constantly. Tucks them under her arm like a student between lectures.

## Personality

> Curious. Earnest. Proud of her position.
>> - She introduces herself as "Sister Eladria, acolyte, cleric of the Lady of the Graves, record keeper of the temple" with a confidence that shines through.
>> - She asks questions. Lots of questions. The kind a scholar asks, not an interrogator.
>> - She is genuinely interested in the party, particularly those who demonstrate knowledge of Pharasmin theology.
>> - She is polite and respectful but not guarded in the way Father Vauran is. She has not yet learned to hold people at arm's length.

## Role in Raven Grow

> Sister Eladria maintains the temple's historical archives and handles correspondence, inventory, and administrative records.
>> - She is not senior enough to perform funeral rites or make decisions about temple property.
>> - She defers to Father Vauran on matters of authority.
>> - She is the person most likely to help the party access the temple's records, should they need to research the town's history.

## First Meeting

> The party met Sister Eladria outside the temple on Rova 11th (Day 1).
>> - Carnon greeted her with a formal Pharasmin address of extraordinary precision, correctly identifying her rank and performing the gesture of candour.
>> - She was visibly impressed and flustered.
>> - She offered to take the party to Father Vauran when Carnon asked about commissioning an enchanted statue to honour Professor Umi-Brelun.
