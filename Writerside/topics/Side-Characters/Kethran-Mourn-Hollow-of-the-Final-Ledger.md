# Kethran Mourn-Hollow of the Final Ledger

A psychopomp who serves at the Church of Pharasma in Magnimar.

## Appearance

Kethran appears as a plague doctor statue that moves with fluid, otherworldly grace.

- Masked face elongated into a ceramic beak
- Body wrapped in layered robes that move independent of wind
- Carries a staff topped with a soul gem housed in a delicate brass framework.
- The gem contains pale lights that drift like drowsy fireflies
- Carries an old brass clockwork timepiece on a silver chain

## Role

Kethran serves as a guide within the Church of Pharasma in Magnimar. He led the Heroes of Magnimar through the church to the Garden of Endings to meet with the High Mourner Vara Lamm.
