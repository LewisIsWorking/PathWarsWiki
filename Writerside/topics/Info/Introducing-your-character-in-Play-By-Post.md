# Introducing-your-character-in-Play-By-Post

> Steps.
>> - Go to the "chat" topic of the campaign you want to join/participate in/start a character in.
>> - Introduce your character to the other players in the chat NOT the play by post topic YET.
>> - Talk over how best to introduce your character with the GM and other players.
>> - Once you have agreed on how to introduce your character, write the introduction post in the play by post topic.
>> - Post the introduction post in the play by post topic.
>> - After posting the introduction post, inform the GM and other players in the chat topic that your character has been introduced.
>> - The GM and other players can then respond to your character's introduction in the play by post topic.
>> - Continue roleplaying your character in the play by post topic.
>> - Remember to stay in character and follow the rules of the campaign.
>> - Have fun!
>> - If you have any questions or need help, ask the GM or other players in the chat topic.
>> - Enjoy your time in the campaign!
