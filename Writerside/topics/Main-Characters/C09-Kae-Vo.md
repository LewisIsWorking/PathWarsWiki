# Kae-Vo (The Kaepra-Voliax Symposium)

> Campaign: C09 Metal City Stargazers.
>> - Player: Cobalt Quail.
>> - System: Starfinder 2e.

## Background

Kae-Vo is a merged barathu collective.
Over a dozen individual entities formed into one floating mass.
Semi-transparent form with prismatic flashes of internal debate.
Multiple tendrils tipped with keratin.
Communicates via telepathy. The telepathic voice sounds like a dozen scholars murmuring in a library, with one voice carrying above the rest.
Refers to themselves as "we" and speaks in formal, academic tones.

## The Symposium

The Kaepra-Voliax Symposium was called to study temporal anomalies and paradoxes.
The collective shares knowledge and reaches consensus before acting.
Excellent at gathering knowledge.
Terrible at making fast decisions.
Discourse and caution are their default states.
