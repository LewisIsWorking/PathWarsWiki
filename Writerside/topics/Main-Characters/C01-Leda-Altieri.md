Current Age: 24

Leda was born in Katheer as the second daughter of a Chelaxian merchant who settled in the city. 

His nephilim ancestry quickly became clear with the golden metallic color of his eyes and hair and the angelic halo that rested over his head. Leda was handed over to the Sarenrae temple closest to her home at age 8 to be raised by the clergy, but she still maintained contact with her family.
