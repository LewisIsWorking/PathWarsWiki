Backstory.

Question: Finally, what is your character's backstory?
Answer: Alitheia is a parthenogenic hatchling. Her village was burned to the ground by unknown assailants and recovered by a group of Sarenite and Desnite travelers who saw the smoke rising from the ruins some days later and investigated. She was raised by the clergy of Sarenrae and came to embrace their ideals of justice. She trained in mundane healing methods as she did not initially have any magical aptitude. As a teenager she began to accompany some of the other clergy members to assist in providing medical aid, and eventually with the cleansing of evildoers. She studied military tactics on her own when she could. While leading her first pilgrimage as a full member of the clergy, she lost consciousness due to unknown forces, and awakened three years later in Sevenarches.

Backstory by each Year.

4687: Egg recovered by Sarenite and Desnite travelers. Egg hatches on the summer solstice that year, 21st of Sarenith.
4688-4696: Early childhood.
4697-4699: Begins to learn first aid and other basic medical skills.
4700-4706: Begins to learn battlefield triage and basic combat training. Begins to accompany other clergy on pilgrimage as an apprentice.
4707: Gains first level of commander. Loses consciousness while leading their first pilgrimage as a full clergy member.
4708-4710: Time lost due to unknown forces. Wakes up in Sevenarches in 4710.
