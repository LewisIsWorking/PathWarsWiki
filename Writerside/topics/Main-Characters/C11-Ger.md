# C11-Ger

> Overview.
>> - Character name: Ger.
>> - Ancestry: Fetchling Skeleton.
>> - Classes: Swarm Master / Decay Instinct Barbarian / Monk.
>> - Player: Paddy.
>> - Current location: River-Way, Nidal, Golarion.

> Backstory.
>> - Ger awoke several years ago in a coffin deep in a cave in a hill near River-Way.
>> - He had no name and no memory.
>> - When attempts to break out of his coffin failed, the unnamed skeleton tried to use his void shell to communicate with anything that might help.
>> - The bugs inside and around the coffin initially rejected his control.
>> - Eventually they relented — eating through the wood to nest inside him.
>> - With his new swarm in place, Ger used his raw strength to burst the coffin open.
>> - The front panel flew off.
>> - Reading the remains of the lid, he found three letters carved in Shadowtongue: "ger."
>> - His name contains those letters sequentially. So his name is Ger.
>> - Ger made his way down the hill into River-Way.
>> - A capable monk, he sought structure and joined the monastery of Zon-Kuthon.
>> - He trained there for several years before the party recruited him in session 01.
>> - Ger carries a mysterious curse: his Decay Instinct.
>> - When his anger spills out, it kills everything around him.
>> - Years of meditation have kept him zen.
>> - Whether that peace will hold now that he walks with the party remains to be seen.
