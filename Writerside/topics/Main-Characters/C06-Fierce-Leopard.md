# Fierce Leopard Cyrus

**Player:** Ryo (@RyoYamakawa)
**Campaign:** #6 Kibwe

---

## Overview

Fierce Leopard Cyrus is a martial combatant known for his devastating strikes and fearless approach to combat.

---

## Key Events

### The White Flame Slime

Fierce Leopard delivered the killing blow to the White Flame Merchant Slime.
When the slime attempted to teleport, Tal'lysae shouted for him to step into the portal.
He did, striking the slime's core and destroying it.

The portal contact teleported Fierce Leopard to an extraplanar space.

### The Vision

Fierce Leopard appeared on a crumbling platform in the void.
He met the ghost of Bashevis, a Taralu dwarf cleric.
Bashevis showed him something that shook him deeply.

Fierce Leopard returned to Kibwe after only seconds of real time, though he experienced several minutes.
He crashed back on his hands and knees, covered in slime, blood, and vomit.

When Tal'lysae asked what he saw, he replied: "Fuck off."

He has not yet shared the details of his vision with the party.

### Crushing Rune

Fierce Leopard's weapon applies Clumsy 1 and Enfeebled 1 on a successful Strike.
These conditions last until the end of the attacker's next turn (not the start).
