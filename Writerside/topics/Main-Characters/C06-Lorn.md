## Backstory

The lore in a nutshell is he was part of the gnoll tribe from the asmodeus hellstorm thing.
He was kidnapped by the black dominion and turned into an eldritch slave.
Selenor finds him and saves him through necromancy.
He spends 1h with Selenor, but he has a hunger for wandering the world, therefore, he leaves him and ends up in Kibwe.

The contract with Ademe dispelled Lorn's Grand Explorer tattoo.

---

## Connection to Selenor and the Grey Flame

Lorn is the son of Selenor (the Wandering Chronicler).
He carries crystals that are connected to Selenor's divine nature.
He views Selenor as family. The Grey Flame was an official way of showing that family bond.
Selenor never forced Lorn to do anything.

Lorn defends his father passionately. He believes Selenor is different from the rest of his family. He believes Selenor can be saved.

Bashevis (the Taralu dwarf ghost) said: "We lost Lorn, our numbers are few."
This implies the Grey Flame considers Lorn lost to its cause, even though Lorn is alive and well in Kibwe.

## Key Events in Part 3

### Conversation with Tal'lysae

Tal'lysae expressed jealousy of Lorn's freedom and sadness that his destiny lies elsewhere.
She looked at his crystals and said: "Not all of us were as forever bound as one would expect."

Lorn asked directly if she was enslaved against her will.
Tal'lysae deflected but admitted she was not granted the same freedom Lorn was.

Lorn offered: "Why not ask my father for help? He is more reasonable than the rest of his family."

### The Watchtower

Lorn said: "I am not a mage. I just shoot my bow."
May feel useless during the ritual decision.
