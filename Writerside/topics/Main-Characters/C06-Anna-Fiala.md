# C06-Anna-Fiala

> Backstory. 
>> 1. She was more or less completely normal girl in her youth, then left to get in a wizard school but got kicked out as a "hopeless and talentless", swear to herself to show them all and went adventuring. 
>> 2. Some months later, she got in a dungeon looking for some ancient magical secrets, triggered a trap that crushed the ceiling on her and turned her lower right arm to pulp. 
>> 3. Thankfully, before she was able to die from the bloodloss, mechanism she was trying to activate, went live, and one of the parts on it (mechanical arm) skittered to her, sliced the remains of the limb and neatly placed itself at her elbow (and maybe put in some more wires in her so ripping it away seemed like a horrible idea).
