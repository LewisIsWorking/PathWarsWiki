Backstory.

Question: Finally, what is your character's backstory?

Answer: The firstborn daughter of a Taldan knightly house, she saw the survival of her house at risk when her parents were unable to have any other children beyond her, let alone a son to take up its knightly banner. Despite the patriarchal society of Taldor, she tried to step into the shoes of her father and become a knight. She did, however, lack any talent for being a knight. One day, when going through the treasury of her family, she found an old banner of her family that seemed to call out to her. When she picked it up, she found out that it was in fact the Intelligent Weapon Bannerlord that forged a pact with her. It would make her a great knight in return for her allowing it to fulfil its purpose again. As a result, she suddenly improved quickly and soon became a knight. After bringing her family honor and glory for a few years, her parents finally managed to have a second child. A son. The moment her brother was born, her father focused entirely on him and seemed to have basically forgotten about her. Eventually this led to an argument between the two in which she accidentally revealed the secret to her prowess. Her father, appalled by her deception and believing that she had forged a pact not with an Intelligent Weapon but some sort of witch-patron named her "Bannerwitch", exiled her and spread what he thought to be true about her among the nobility.
