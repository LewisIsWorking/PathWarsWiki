# Nariya

> Overview.
>> - Character name: Nariya.
>> - Ancestry: Nightmare Dragon Vampire.
>> - Classes: Untamed Druid / Kineticist (dual gate: water and fire).
>> - Player: Namyra.
>> - Age: 280.
>> - Current location: River-Way, Nidal, Golarion.

> Appearance.
>> - 162cm — smaller than most dragons of her age.
>> - Slender build, longer torso than legs.
>> - Black spines running from neck down to tail.
>> - Two slender arms, two stockier legs. Long thick black claws.
>> - Scales are chameleonic — shift colour to match surroundings. Sparkle in sunlight.
>> - Bat-like wings. 24ft wingspan.
>> - Slender but solid tail with a black arrowhead spine at the tip.
>> - Lizard-like face. Two markhor-style black horns, roughly 12 inches each.
>> - Large snakelike eyes — light green when fed, blood red when hungry. Glow in darkness.
>> - Razor-sharp teeth and a very long forked tongue.
>> - Bite marks on her neck from a vampire bat encountered in a cave.
>> - A large spider bite scar on her left wrist.
>> - Wears a silver collar engraved with a flame symbol and a water wave symbol — forged by her druid mentor.
>> - Blank or deeply thoughtful at rest. Full of rage in battle.
>> - A calming presence in nature. Constantly reads her surroundings.
>> - Fidgety — sways her tail, sharpens her claws. Visibly anxious in unnatural environments.

> Backstory.
>> - Nariya's egg was laid in a magic-saturated volcano — the origin of her fire elemental power.
>> - The volcano erupted and launched the egg into the ocean.
>> - A storm blessed the egg with a connection to water and carried it into a small cave.
>> - During a nightmare storm, lightning struck the egg. She hatched as a Nightmare Dragon.
>> - No mother. No father. Alone in the cave.

> The Dusk Forest.
>> - Nariya ventured out of the cave into the nearby Dusk Forest.
>> - A lone druid found her, initially prepared to attack.
>> - The forest itself spoke to the druid — told them this dragon needed guidance.
>> - The druid raised her among animals and beasts.
>> - She was taught language, crafting, occultism, acrobatics, stealth, natural magic, and the importance of balance.
>> - Unlike most dragons she had no interest in treasure or wealth — she craved knowledge of the natural world and magic.
>> - Animals trusted her. In time she could shapeshift and communicate across many species.
>> - Her giant great horned owl has been her constant companion ever since.
>> - The druid had a silver collar forged for her — flame and water wave symbols — to help her channel her elemental abilities.

> The Nightmare Curse.
>> - Her nightmare powers came with a cost she did not choose.
>> - She unwillingly entered and created nightmares in others — a strange reality she could not fully understand or control.

> The Spider and the Bat.
>> - Age 56: a miscommunication with a spider. Bitten on the left wrist.
>> - Spider venom has been in her bloodstream ever since.
>> - Decades later, Nariya investigated dark cursed caves.
>> - A large vampire bat bit her and passed on the vampire curse.
>> - She suffered through the transformation and woke with a powerful hunger for blood.
>> - She now asks permission where possible, or feeds from those who harm the natural world.

> Meeting Atticus.
>> - Nariya encountered an automaton — something wholly unnatural, deeply at odds with her beliefs.
>> - Atticus saved her life.
>> - This forced her to reconsider: not all that is unnatural is harmful.
>> - An unlikely bond formed. One forged by nature. One forged by war.
>> - They have travelled together since.

> Session Moments.
>> - Session 02: Helped de-escalate the standoff at Malia's house — threw 20 GP to calm things down.
>> - Session 02: Her nature as a nightmare dragon of shadow, fire and water allowed the party to fully activate the Grief Glass.
>> - Session 02: Took part in the Grief Glass reading — the combined power of shadow, fire and water revealed that someone is sacrificing people to widen the planar rifts beneath River Way.
