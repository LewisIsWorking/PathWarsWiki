Backstory.
Finally, what is your character’s backstory?
4705 AR - Egg was found among debris from a flood along the Yondabakari River in Varisia. Hatched a few days later on 2 Calistril. Taken into the Dewfall Orphanage.
4706 AR - No major events of note
4707 AR - Ori Viviede moved to Dewfall and started teaching music to any who had an interest.
4708 AR - Kitt reaches their juvenile state, their growth slowing considerably. Begins helping with basic chores in the kitchen.
4709 AR - Ori Viviede along with a couple of travelling adventurers fended off an attack on the village by goblins. Kitt began learning music from Ori.
4710 AR - Starts learning how to cook basic items from the Matron beginning their love of cooking and especially baking. Magical mishaps start happening around them, eventually leading to being taught how to control their innate magic.
4711 AR - A small festival celebrating the village’s founding is held. The children at the orphanage perform a song during the festivities.
4712 AR - Learned how to make their first suncatcher with help from Ori.
4713 AR - A fire burns down the orphanage. Luckily no one died, but it traumatized many of the children Kitt included. With help from the entire village a new orphanage is built.
4714 AR - Having learned about their own magic and the magic of song Kitt starts to be tutored in the ways of the Bard.
4715 AR - An illness spreads amongst the villagers, several older citizens and two children die from it.
4716 AR - A vision of rainbows and light constantly appears in their dreams. Ori and the matron eventually find out that it is possibly related to the Empyrean Lord, Lalaci. Ori, as a follower of Lalaci, knows some of their teachings but does not know the ways of the clergy so a combined effort from the two motherly figures in their life starts to help them towards the path of a Cleric.
4717 AR - Having reached adulthood the young Kitt leaves the village behind and heads to Magnimar where a friend of Ori’s lives and can continue their tutelage.
4718 - 4719 AR - Spends the following two years learning and fine-tuning their skills while taking on odd jobs. Joined the Guard on a whim after overhearing a conversation about them recruiting talented people with diverse talents.
