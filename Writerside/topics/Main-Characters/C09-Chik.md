## Background

Chik is a Ysoki (rat-folk).
Small, quick, sharp-toothed.
Raised in a creche. References "Grandmother Niska" and creche signals.
Follows Quick-Paw (personal deity or spirit).
Detailed, descriptive writer. Long RP posts.

## Key Moments

> The Scream.
>> - Chik felt something fundamentally change when the Scream hit.
>> - Woke in a separate cell from the main party.
>> - First awareness: his rifle was gone. Felt the absence like a phantom limb.
>> - His force needle was still concealed in its forearm sheath. It felt patient. Waiting.

> The Brig.
>> - Tapped on the cell door with an old creche signal (three quick, pause, two more).
>> - Called out to whoever was outside.
>> - Joo-Dee recognized Chik and opened his cell.

> Gearing Up.
>> - Immediately retrieved his Magnetar rifle from the smuggling compartment.
>> - Pressed his forehead against it: "Hey, buddy. Missed you too."
>> - Stored Dekker's communicator in his right cheek pouch.
>> - Stored hacking toolkit in his left cheek pouch.
>> - Stripped the estex suit off Dekker's corpse, rolled it, stowed it.
>> - Activated Shift Immanence. Awakened the Unfailing Bow ikon in the rifle.
>> - Positioned behind Joo-Dee as cover, rifle charged and ready.
