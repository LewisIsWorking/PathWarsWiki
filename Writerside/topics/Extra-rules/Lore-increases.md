# Lore Increases

A lore increase is a skill increase that can only be applied to lore skills.

Every time you get a skill increase as part of your character's class progression, you get a lore increase.

This also applies to Rogues with their increased frequency of skill increases.

Rogues get a lore increase every time they get a skill increase.

## Proficiency Gates

- Expert: Any level.
- Master: Level 7+.
- Legendary: Level 15+.

## Lore Increases by Level

- 3: Expert.
- 5: Expert.
- 7: Master.
- 9: Master.
- 11: Master.
- 13: Master.
- 15: Legendary.
- 17: Legendary.
- 19: Legendary.

## How Many Lores Do You Have?

Assumes one Trained lore from your background.
Each increase can raise an existing lore by one step (within the level gates) or open a new lore at Trained.

| Level | Total Increases | Focus Path                        | Spread Path   |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------------------------|---------------|
| 1     | 0               | 1x Trained                        | 1x Trained    |
| 3     | 1               | 1x Expert                         | 2x Trained    |
| 5     | 2               | 1x Expert, 1x Trained             | 3x Trained    |
| 7     | 3               | 1x Master, 1x Trained             | 4x Trained    |
| 9     | 4               | 1x Master, 1x Expert              | 5x Trained    |
| 11    | 5               | 1x Master, 1x Master              | 6x Trained    |
| 13    | 6               | 1x Master, 1x Master, 1x Trained  | 7x Trained    |
| 15    | 7               | 1x Legendary, 1x Master, 1x Trained | 8x Trained  |
| 17    | 8               | 2x Legendary, 1x Trained          | 9x Trained    |
| 19    | 9               | 2x Legendary, 1x Expert           | 10x Trained   |

Focus path: stack each lore as high as the level gates allow before opening the next.
Spread path: open a new lore at Trained with every increase.
