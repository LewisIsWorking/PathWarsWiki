# Lore Increases

A lore increase is a skill increase that can only be applied to lore skills.

Every time you get a skill increase as part of your character's class progression, you get a lore increase.

This also applies to Rogues with their increased frequency of skill increases.

Rogues get a lore increase every time they get a skill increase.

## Proficiency by Level

- 3: Expert.
- 5: Expert.
- 7: Master.
- 9: Master.
- 11: Master.
- 15: Legendary.
- 17: Legendary.
- 19: Legendary.

## How Many Lores Do You Have?

Assumes one Trained lore from your background.
Each lore increase can raise an existing lore by one step, or start a new lore at Trained.

| Level | Total Increases | If You Focus (one lore deep) | If You Spread (max different lores) |
|-------|-----------------|------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| 1     | 0               | 1x Trained                   | 1x Trained                          |
| 3     | 1               | 1x Expert                    | 2x Trained                          |
| 5     | 2               | 1x Master                    | 3x Trained                          |
| 7     | 3               | 1x Legendary                 | 4x Trained                          |
| 9     | 4               | 1x Legendary, 1x Trained     | 5x Trained                          |
| 11    | 5               | 1x Legendary, 1x Expert      | 6x Trained                          |
| 15    | 6               | 1x Legendary, 1x Master      | 7x Trained                          |
| 17    | 7               | 2x Legendary                 | 8x Trained                          |
| 19    | 8               | 2x Legendary, 1x Trained     | 9x Trained                          |
