# The Silver Succubi Shop

> **Type:** Outfitters / Smithy / Arcane counter (inside the Silver Succubi).
> **Location:** Riddleport, Varisia. Back-of-house of the Silver Succubi casino.
> **Owner:** Marlo Kaspar Viktor Cyrus (staffed by Davy Crab-Basket and Mira Ashvane).

---

## Overview

> The shop is Marlo's back-of-house outfitters, tucked off the casino floor of the Silver Succubi.
>> - Marlo grants his crews access to it when they are working a job for him.
>> - It exists so his hired hands can appraise, sell, re-arm, and upgrade without leaving his roof (or his cut).
>> - The party were sent here to appraise and sell the haul from cleaning out the church, and to re-gear before the hit on Anger.

---

## The Room

> A single room split cleanly down the middle, one trade to each side, with a shopkeeper for each.
>> - **Left (Martial):** weapons, armour, martial and physical items. Davy Crab-Basket's counter.
>> - **Right (Arcane):** gems, rings, amulets, bags, wands, staves, scrolls, and magic-focused items. Mira Ashvane's counter.
>> - Two separate counters, each with a door behind it leading to the owner's workroom.
>> - Several bells sound when the shop door opens. One shopkeeper emerges from each door to meet customers.

---

## Davy Crab-Basket (Martial Counter)

> The Silver Succubi's one-eyed orc head of security, doubling as the shop's smith.
>> - Older, mean, and a mid-tier fighter who could still kill most people quickly if not for a dodgy knee, bad back, old age, and the missing eye.
>> - Bouncing doors is hard on that knee, so the forge suits him. The work is slow but genuinely good.
>> - Professional and to the point. He knows the crew are on a big job the boss is keeping close to his chest, and he expects it to get rough.

> **His craft.**
>> - Everything Davy makes is in the orcish style, from orcish materials: dark green and vicious-looking.
>> - Weapons, armour, rune-etching, repairs, and on-the-spot upgrades.

> **Sample voice.**
>> - "Ah. I was told you are all part of the crew. I know the boss has given you a big job, close to his chest. I know it'll involve getting rough. What can I smith for you?"

---

## Mira Ashvane (Arcane Counter)

> The red-haired, fire-magic Cyphermage who runs the magic side.
>> - A Cyphermage initiate, formerly seen only as a corner-table regular buried in books and papers, nursing a single drink for hours.
>> - Wears an amber pendant at her throat, the colour of fire and embers.
>> - Researches Sin glyphs and transformation magic. Uninterested in gambling, drinking, or entertainment.
>> - Prickly and unamused by the party, whom she treats as a recurring interruption to her studies.

> **Her stock and services.**
>> - Identification and appraisal of magic items, plus scrolls, wands, staves, rings, amulets, gems, bags, and consumables.
>> - Enchanting and arcane upgrade work, her specialty being glyphs and transformation.

> **Sample voice.**
>> - "It did *not* take very long for you to bother me again."

---

## Goods, Services, and Selling

> Appraise and fence the church haul, then re-gear for Anger.
>> - **Martial (Davy):** sells weapons/armour, etches runes, repairs and upgrades gear, buys mundane and martial loot.
>> - **Arcane (Mira):** identifies and appraises magic items, sells arcane consumables and implements, does enchanting.
>> - **Fencing:** Riddleport asks no questions about provenance. Mundane goods move at roughly half Price; Marlo's stake plus a good Diplomacy/Intimidation nudge can push the crew's rate a little higher.
>> - **Buying cap:** items up to the party's usual band, plus one tier Marlo's access unlocks that they could not normally walk in and buy. [TODO: set the exact level/rarity cap for this party.]

## GM Notes and Hooks

> **The right buyer for the weird stuff.**
>> - Mira researches Sin glyphs and transformation magic, and the party is selling loot from a church.
>> - If any of that haul is glyph-marked, sin-themed, or transformation-flavoured, Mira wants it badly: she will pay over the odds, or trade scroll-stock, services, or Cyphergate intel, for first crack at studying it.
>> - This hands the players a lever: Davy fences the plain steel at the usual rate, but the one person in Riddleport who specifically wants the strange relics is standing at the other counter. Describe her amber pendant catching the light when she gets excited.

> **Davy respects competence.**
>> - A crew that clearly knows its business earns better rates and the odd favour. Time-wasters pay the "idiot tax."

> **Canon note.**
>> - Mira was previously listed only as a Regular Patron of the Silver Succubi; running the arcane counter is a promotion of that role.
>> - Spelling: this page uses "Ashvane" per the current in-game posts; the side-characters entry spells it "Ash-vane." Worth normalising to one.
