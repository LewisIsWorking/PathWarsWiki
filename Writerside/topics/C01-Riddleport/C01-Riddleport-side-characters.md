# C01-Riddleport-side-characters


## Key NPCs

### Management

> Marlo Kaspar Viktor Cyrus
>> - **Owner**
>> - A short man in his fifties wearing a formal suit of black silk.
>> - Thinning black hair slicked back.
>> - His left arm ends just above the wrist, replaced with a masterwork clockwork prosthetic of brass and steel.
>> - The mechanical hand eternally clutches a skeleton key of black iron covered in shifting runic engravings.
>> - Everyone calls him "Marlo" or "Mr. Cyrus" (formal).
>> - Don't stare at the hand.
>> - Don't ask about it.
>> - Don't make hand jokes.
>> - Marlo runs a "legitimate" business by Riddleport standards.
>> - This makes him controversial among crime lords for his non-violent business approach.

> Grudge Iron-bellow.
>> - **Floor Manager (Dwarf)**
>> - Runs the day-to-day operations.
>> - Dour, efficient, holds grudges (hence the name).
>> - Has a grudge against Beltias Kreun for cheating the bounder tables and escaping before punishment could be administered.

> Karnath
>> - **Personal Bodyguard / Beast-Wrangler**
>> - Goes by one name only.
>> - Marlo's personal muscle.
>> - Handles the Octahedron fighting pit in the basement.
>> - Drinks too much, but fights like a demon when sober.

### Bar Staff

> Callie Duskbarrel
>> - **Head Barmaid / Greeter**
>> - Half-elven woman in her thirties (hard to tell with elven blood).
>> - Wears the succubus uniform like a uniform of office rather than seduction.
>> - Has been doing this job too long to care about looking seductive.
>> - Knows everyone. Remembers everything. Maternal in a tough-love way.
>> - Has history with Lenma Moonshine - thinks of her as a lovable disaster.

> Scarlet Vayne
>> - **Barmaid (Part-Time)**
>> - Fiery red-haired woman, good with a sword.
>> - Grew up as a bastard, mocked as a child.
>> - Recently discovered her father's name: Beltias Kreun.
>> - Bears no love for him and intends to gut him publicly.

> Jorrik
>> - **Bartender**
>> - Older human man.
>> - Picks pockets on the side.
>> - Knows everyone's secrets. Keeps them... for the right price.

> Maelis
>> - **Bartender**
>> - Half-elf, young, actually honest.
>> - Pretty but terrible at the job.
>> - Probably won't last long, but customers like looking at her.

### Security

> Torval and Brick
>> - **Vault Guards**
>> - The two most trusted guards at the Silver Succubi.
>> - Watch over the cashier's cage and vault during operating hours.
>> - Professional, reliable, don't ask questions.

> Davy Crab-Basket
>> - **Door Bouncer**
>> - One-eyed orc.
>> - Older.
>> - Mean.
>> - Mid-tier fighter who could kill most people quickly... if not for his dodgy knee, bad back, old age, and missing eye.

### Gaming Staff

> Whisper Vex
>> - **Ghoulette Operator**
>> - Opportunistic rogue who discovered the magic talking head (Dungo) in a graveyard.
>> - Invented the Ghoulette game and brought it to the Silver Succubi.
>> - Clever, quiet, always watching.

> Teeth
>> - **Cashier**
>> - Goblin woman.
>> - Called "Teeth" because she's missing most of hers.
>> - Fast with numbers, faster with insults.

### Entertainment

> Old Scratch
>> - **Caged Imp**
>> - Paying off a debt to Marlo by performing at the establishment.
>> - Cantankerous, vulgar, entertaining.
>> - Screams obscenities and insults patrons.
>> - Secretly misses Hell but would never admit it.

## Regular Patrons

> Mira Ash-vane.
>> - **Cypher mage Initiate**
>> - Often found at a corner table, surrounded by books and papers.
>> - Nurses a single drink for hours while she studies.
>> - Wears an amber pendant at her throat - the colour of fire, the colour of embers.
>> - Researches Sin glyphs and transformation magic.
>> - One of the few people in the establishment who seems completely uninterested in gambling, drinking or entertainment.

## Connections to Beltias Kreun

> Multiple parties have interest in Beltias Kreun:
>> - **Grudge Iron-bellow** - Wants him punished for cheating the bounder tables.
>> - **Scarlet Vayne** - His bastard daughter who wants to gut him publicly.
>> - **Cap'n "Keelhaul" Varne** - Ship captain with a bounty on Beltias's head for stealing charts and/or his Varisian dancing girl.
>> - **Corsair Blacktide** - Smuggling crimelord whose couriers have been jumped by Beltias's gang.
>> - **The Party** - Captured him and delivered him to the Silver Succubi.
